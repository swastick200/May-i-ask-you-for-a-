To the Beautiful Kakima
Laher jaise baheti ho zulfein tumhari asmani,
Jheel si gehri aankhon mein basi ek kahani.
Awaz tumhari jaise subah ki shabnami,
Hothon pe muskaan, gulabon ki rawani.

Palkon ke jadu se dil ko behlati ho,
Kajal ke teeron se bechuk nishana lagati ho.
Nagin si adaayein, dil chura le jaati ho,
Bin kahe dhadkanon ka raaz ban jaati ho.

Har andaaz tumhara ek jadoo sa lagta hai,
Khuda ke afsane ka hissa sa lagta hai.
Ek hum hi hain jo tumpe poore ke poore marte hain,
Warna kisi ko tumhari aankhein kisiko laheza marta hai.
Dedicated to the one who makes hearts skip a beat.
